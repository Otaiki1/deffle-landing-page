About

Deffle is a decentralized raffle platform that is powered by AI and managed on the blockchain. It enables businesses, organizations, and people to launch their own raffles and interact with participants using the most reliable and well-recognized technology.

Smart

Raffles are powered by smart contracts that are built on the Polygon blockchain, enabling countless quick and transparent transactions.

Reliable

Since the platform is decentralized, players, charity, and raffle operators can all use it freely, anonymously, and securely.

Inclusive

The blockchain is used for everything, making it cross-border and available to anybody with an internet connection. It also has a social component by enabling group raffle play.

Addressing issues to seize the expanding market

The raffle industry has grown from a $300 billion industry to one that is expected to reach $393 billion by 2023 thanks to the common illusion of sudden riches.
But the sector faces some significant issues:
- Location-based restrictions on options
- Small and sporadic jackpots
- Trust-eroding scams
- Poor user experience due to hidden costs and sluggish payouts - In the age of social media, there has been no innovation to promote social interaction
